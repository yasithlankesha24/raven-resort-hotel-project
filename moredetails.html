Pool

Take a refreshing dip in one of our luxurious swimming pools when the tropical sun reaches its highest during the middle of the day. At the Raven Resort in Unawatuna, guests can enjoy unlimited use of two stunning swimming pools.

A great place to begin your exploration of the resort is the Beach Club Pool located on-site. The pool, which is 154 square meters, is a great spot to relax and take in the salty sea air. As the day draws to a close and night falls, the music at the beach club gets louder and louder. Take a dip in our infinity pool and dance all night to the pulsating rhythms of our in-house DJ.

SRILANKA - AN ISLAND OF PARADISE

The word 'paradise' is bandied around endlessly by travellers: it's used to describe every stretch of whitesand beach or rainforest they see. But while modern tourists might write '#paradise' alongside each snap they post on Instagram, there's a genuine case for Sri Lanka's identification as Paradise on Earth, thanks to a series of literary references.
